About LSA


Los Santos Aviation (LSA), a part of the San Andreas Aviation Administration (SAAA), plays a vital role in civil aviation across the state. LSA offers pilot licensing through structured exams and professional training conducted by Certified Flight Instructors (CFI).

​Citizens are warmly welcomed to become a part of the aviation community, learning to navigate the skies while upholding the highest standards of safety and expertise.

LSA is dedicated to inspiring a passion for aviation and cultivating a skilled network of pilots for a thriving aviation future.
