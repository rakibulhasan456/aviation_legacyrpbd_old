AVIATION INFORMATION

HELI
A Private helicopter License authorizes you to operate helicopters without the supervision of an instructor under the terms of your license, LSA regulations. Upon receiving your Type-Heli license you will be rated to fly helicopters classified as “Light weight”.
FIXED WING
A Private Pilot’s License authorizes you to operate aircraft without the supervision of an instructor under the terms of your license, LSA regulations. Upon receiving your Type-FW license you will be rated to fly fixed wing aircraft classified as “Light weight”.
HEAVYWEIGHT
If you complete the Heavyweight course and pass the exam, you will be eligible for the Heavyweight license.
COMMERCIAL
If you complete CPL (Commercial Pilot) course, eligible for commercial license.
CFI
This license allows you to provide Instruction/ Training sessions & LSA Pilot recruitment exams. Upon receiving your CFI license your call-sign will be appended with “C” allowing other pilots to recognize your current rating. Performance license will be included in this.
